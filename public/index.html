About Me

My name is Jesse Kearl and I am currently a resident of Lethbridge, Alberta. This coming fall I am finishing my last semester of my Bachelor of Science in Mathematics at the University of Lethbridge. I discovered my love of software development later on in my degree and have been learning as much as I can ever since. In my free time I enjoy rock climbing, chess, and playing retro video games.

My Software Philosophy

I believe aesthetics are very important in software and I would also consider myself a minimalist. The best software doesn't overwhelm users by presenting them with too much too soon. Software should be intuitive and as it's made it's important to consider first those who are the least comfortable with the technology. Software should make the world a better place and not bring stress and anxiety to people. It should bring people joy, and I try in some small way to contribute to that.

Jesse Kearl, 2021
